Welcome to the Additive Design and Manufacturing Lab

Here at ADML, we mainly focus on researching Additive Manufacturing (AM) and related technologies. ADML is affiliated with the Department of Mechanical Engineering at McGill University in Montréal, Québec, Canada. A team of graduate and undergraduate students led by a professor who specializes in design and manufacturing research is working on cutting-edge technologies and research projects. This is possible thanks to our numerous sponsors and partners both within the faculty and from industries throughout the world.
